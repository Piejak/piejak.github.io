Metric Clock

This is a clock that shows what the time and date would be if the world ran on a metric, meaning base 10, time system.

The length of the year is the exact same as normal time.

There are 30 metric weeks in a year.

There are 10 metric days in a metric week.

There are 10 metric hours in a metric day.

There are 100 metric minutes in a metric hour.

There are 100 metric seconds in a metric minute.

A metric second is 1.0512 normal seconds.
